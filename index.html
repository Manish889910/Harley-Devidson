KEY FEATURES

Air/oil-cooled 440 cc, long-stroke, two-valve engine

The Harley-Davidson X440 is powered by an air/oil-cooled 440 cc, long-stroke, two-valve engine12345. The engine produces a maximum output of 27 bhp at 6,000 rpm and 38 Nm of peak torque at 4,000 rpm4312. The engine is said to offer a flat torque curve with 90 per cent of its peak torque from as low as 2,000 rpm1. The motor is mated to a six-speed gearbox and gets a slip and assist clutch

harley davidson x440 build and comfort

The well-cushioned split seat provides ample support for both rider and passenger during long journeys. Wide and flat handlebars offer a commanding rider position. Optimized chassis geometry ensures stability at high speeds.In summary, the X440 strikes a balance between performance and comfort, making it an appealing choice for riders seeking a neo-retro roadster experience

Class-Leading Lighting & Technology: The X440 boasts cutting-edge lighting technology, ensuring visibility and safety on the road. Whether cruising at night or during the day, you’ll appreciate the attention to detail in the lighting setup. Signature Sound: When you ride an X440, your arrival is announced by that unmistakable Harley-Davidson exhaust note.

Classic Turtle Tank: The distinctive tank design adds character from every angle, making the X440 instantly recognizable

Confident Handling: The optimized geometry provides a commanding view of the road. You’ll feel in control, whether navigating city streets or cruising on highways.
